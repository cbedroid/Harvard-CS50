What Is Pydatiff?

Pydatpiff is an online music player that lets you listen and download the newest Hip-Hop and RnB song.

If you like listening to music, then you will love this project. With over 100,000 artists and songs from the newest to the oldies. Whether you like 90's, '80's or even 70's music, you will love pydatpiff. With a wide range of genres to choose from, you can listen to songs from Lil Wayne, Tupac, Biggie Smalls down to Michael Jackson,Otis Redding or The Temptations ... and many more.

Over 50 different categories of genre:

  • Hip-Hop
  • RnB
  • Pop
  • Blues
  • Jazz
  • Dance
  • Country
  • Musical theatre
  • Rock-n-Roll
  • Punk
  • Electronic

.. and many more
